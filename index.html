The silent killers

Why visitors leave without booking

Most coaching pages have at least one of these — invisible to the owner, obvious to a visitor.

  • 01 No 'Book Now' button in sight — visitors read everything and have nowhere obvious to go next
  • 02 Pricing is a mystery — so they hesitate or quietly leave
  • 03 Too many competing offers — courses, freebies, podcast, community all fighting for attention at once
  • 04 Broken on mobile — 80%+ of your Instagram traffic is on a phone. Most coaching pages look rough there
  • 05 Zero social proof — no testimonials, no client results. Cold visitors need a reason to trust you first
